Project

eye appealing, simple, modular PHP captcha

An easy to setup captcha system written in PHP that works out of the box. All parameters are optional, it just works. Feel free to supply optional parameters to tweak it to your liking. No PHP knowledge required to integrate and modify this captcha.

The demo built for this captcha is a simple email form prettified with Twitter Bootstrap (http://twitter.github.com/bootstrap) and made more fuctional with jQuery (http://jquery.com)
